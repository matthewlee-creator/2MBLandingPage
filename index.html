Meet The Artists

FOR STUDENTS, BY STUDENTS

Born in the hallways of Springside Chestnut Hill Academy, 2MB Records is a launchpad for raw talent. We're a student-run collective dedicated to producing, promoting, and celebrating the authentic music created by our peers. From bedroom producers to stage-ready bands, we provide the platform to be heard. Additionally, we give back to the community which shaped us, teaching the next generation music production in collaboration with groups like Horizons.
